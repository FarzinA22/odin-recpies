Scrambled eggs

Some delicious scrambled eggs here, yum!

Ingredients you need

  • 4x egg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • olive oil or butter

Steps for preparaton

  1. Heat up pan over medium heat
  2. put butter/oliveoil before the pan heats up
  3. While the pan is heating up, take your eggs in a bowl and put all of the ingredients in the bowl and mix
  4. pour the mix into pan
  5. stir it until it solidifies
  6. remove it from the heat to your preference
